Lasagna recipe

Description

This easy no boil lasagna recipe uses two meats and three cheeses for amazing flavor. Your favorite jarred spaghetti sauce keeps it simple!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • ½ sweet italian sausage
  • 48 ounces spaghetti sauce
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozarella cheese
  • ¾ grated parmesan
  • 3 teaspoons dried parsely
  • 2 large eggs
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • ¼ cup water

How to

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F.
  2. In a large skillet over medium heat brown the ground beef and sausage together. Drain off and discard any fat.
  3. Add the minced garlic and spaghetti sauce. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  4. In a large bowl, mix together the ricotta, 2 cups of the mozzarella cheese, eggs, ½cup of grated Parmesan cheese, dried parsley, salt and pepper. Mix well.
  5. To assemble, spray a 9×13 baking pan with cooking spray.
  6. Spread ¾ cup of sauce on the bottom of the baking pan.
  7. Cover the bottom with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles.
  8. Top with ⅓ of the cheese mixture and ¼ of the sauce.
  9. Repeat layers (noodles, cheese, sauce, noodles, cheese, sauce).
  10. Top with 3 noodles, remaining sauce, remaining 1 cup of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  11. add the ¼ cup of water.
  12. Spray a sheet of aluminium foil with cooking spray. Cover with the pan with aluminum foil (sprayed side down). This spray prevents the cheese from sticking.
  13. Bake for 50 minutes. Uncover and bake an additional 10 minutes.
  14. Let the lasagna stand for 5 to 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
