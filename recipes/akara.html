Akara recipe

Description

Akara is a popular Nigerian delicacy made with beans mainly black-eyed beans.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups dry black eye beans or peas
  • 4 large onions
  • Vegetable oil (for deep frying)
  • 3 bell peppers
  • Ground red pepper
  • 5 chilli peppers

How to

  1. Wash and deskin beans.
  2. Blend with onions, bell pepper and chilli peppers into a thick paste(paste shouldn't be runny).
  3. Mix paste with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Heat a generous amount of oil for deep frying.
  5. Pour the paste into the oil carefully and fry gently until turns brown
  6. Turn over as needed.
  7. Wait for it too cool and serve.
