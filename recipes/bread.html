Cheesy chicken stuffed bread

Description

This easy stuffed bread recipe with cheese and chicken is fantastic as an easy lunch or dinner

Ingredients

  • Bread dough
  • Shredded cooked chicken
  • Peppers roughly chopped
  • 2 cups rated mozarella
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chilli sauce

How to

  1. Prepare dough according to instructions and leeve to rise.
  2. When the dough has proofed, roll it out into a large rectangle on a floured surface.
  3. Sprinkle over the mozzarella cheese followed by the chicken and peppers and chilli sauce. Season to taste
  4. Roll the dough into a tight roll then place on a baking sheet topped with baking paper.
  5. Allow to proof for another 30 minutes while pre-heating the oven to 180°C.
  6. Place the stuffed bread into the oven and allow to bake for 20-30 minutes until golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Remove from the oven and allow to cool for 5-10 minutes before slicing and serving.
